# Revenue per Truck per Week — Week Ending 2026-02-15

**Period**: 2025-08-02 to 2026-02-15 (26 weeks)

## Executive Summary

Revenue per truck per week (Rev/Trk/Wk) across Central region shows diverging trends: Albuquerque improved efficiency (+6.2% WoW) even as its truck count declined, while Amarillo deteriorated sharply (-18% over 4 weeks) on a larger truck base. Network average Rev/Trk/Wk is $3,847, marginally below the $3,900 prior-year baseline.

## Top Findings

1. **Amarillo — Sharp Revenue Efficiency Drop** (HIGH): Rev/Trk/Wk fell from $4,210 to $3,447 over 4 weeks (-18.1%). Correlation with loaded miles (r = 0.88) suggests shorter haul distances are reducing per-truck revenue. 42 trucks affected.

2. **Albuquerque — Efficiency Gain Offsetting Volume Loss** (MEDIUM): Despite 28 trucks (down from 35), Rev/Trk/Wk rose to $3,921 (+6.2% WoW). Remaining trucks appear to be taking more productive routes.

3. **Troutdale — Consistent Performer** (LOW): Rev/Trk/Wk has stayed in the $4,050–$4,200 range for the full 26-week period. No change points or anomalies detected.
